ACME Software Suite - Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What is the ACME Software Suite?
A: The ACME Software Suite is an integrated platform that provides business management tools including CRM, project management, analytics, and automation capabilities. It's designed to streamline operations for small to medium-sized businesses.

Q: How much does ACME cost?
A: We offer three pricing tiers:
- Starter: $29/month for up to 5 users
- Professional: $79/month for up to 25 users
- Enterprise: $199/month for unlimited users
All plans include core features with increasing storage and advanced functionality.

Q: Is there a free trial available?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Professional tier features. No credit card required to start your trial.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

Q: What browsers are supported?
A: ACME works with all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

Q: Can I integrate ACME with other tools?
A: Absolutely! ACME integrates with over 200 popular business tools including Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, QuickBooks, and Salesforce. Check our integrations page for the complete list.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, conduct regular security audits, and maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance. All data is stored in secure, redundant data centers.

Q: Do you offer mobile apps?
A: Yes, ACME mobile apps are available for iOS and Android devices. The mobile apps provide access to core features optimized for mobile use.

ACCOUNT AND BILLING

Q: How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: You can change your plan anytime from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades occur at your next billing cycle.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can pay by wire transfer or check.

Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?
A: Yes, we provide 30% discounts for qualified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with your organization details to apply.

Q: What happens if I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime. Your account remains active until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, you have 30 days to export your data before it's permanently deleted.

SUPPORT AND TRAINING

Q: What support options are available?
A: We provide multiple support channels:
- 24/7 chat support for all paid plans
- Email support with response within 24 hours
- Phone support for Professional and Enterprise plans
- Comprehensive knowledge base and video tutorials

Q: Do you offer training for new users?
A: Yes, we provide free onboarding sessions for new customers, recorded webinar training, and detailed documentation. Enterprise customers receive dedicated training sessions.

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?
A: You can submit bug reports or feature requests through our support portal at support.acme.com. We review all submissions and provide updates on development progress.

Q: Is there a user community or forum?
A: Yes, join our user community at community.acme.com to connect with other users, share best practices, and get tips from ACME experts.

GETTING STARTED

Q: How long does implementation take?
A: Most customers are up and running within a few hours. Our setup wizard guides you through the initial configuration, and our support team is available to help with data migration and advanced setup.

Q: Can I import data from my current system?
A: Yes, ACME supports data import from most popular business software including Excel, CSV files, and direct migrations from competitors' platforms. Our team can assist with complex migrations.

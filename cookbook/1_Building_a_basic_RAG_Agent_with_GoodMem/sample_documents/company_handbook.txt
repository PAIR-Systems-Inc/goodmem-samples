ACME Corporation Employee Handbook

Welcome to ACME Corporation! This handbook provides essential information about our company policies, procedures, and culture.

COMPANY OVERVIEW
ACME Corporation is a leading technology company founded in 2010, specializing in innovative software solutions for businesses worldwide. Our mission is to empower organizations through cutting-edge technology and exceptional service.

WORK HOURS AND POLICIES
Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We offer flexible working arrangements including remote work options. Employees are expected to maintain professional standards and communicate effectively with their teams.

TIME OFF POLICY
All full-time employees receive:
- 15 days of paid vacation annually (increases to 20 days after 3 years)
- 10 sick days per year
- 8 company holidays
- Personal days as needed with manager approval

Vacation requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Sick leave can be used for personal illness or to care for immediate family members.

BENEFITS PACKAGE
ACME offers comprehensive benefits including:
- Health insurance with 90% premium coverage
- Dental and vision insurance
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match
- Life insurance equal to 2x annual salary
- Employee stock options after 1 year of employment
- Professional development allowance of $2,000 annually

CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must maintain the highest standards of professional ethics. This includes:
- Treating colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintaining confidentiality of proprietary information
- Avoiding conflicts of interest
- Reporting any unethical behavior to management
- Following all safety protocols and procedures

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance evaluations are conducted annually, typically in January. Reviews focus on goal achievement, professional development, and career progression. Employees should prepare self-assessments and discuss career objectives with their managers.
